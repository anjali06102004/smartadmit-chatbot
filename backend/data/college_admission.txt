College Admission Information

Admission Process:
1. Online Application Form submission
2. Document verification and submission
3. Entrance exam (for engineering courses)
4. Interview (for MBA and MCA programs)
5. Merit list publication
6. Fee payment and enrollment

Required Documents:
- 10th and 12th mark sheets
- Transfer certificate from previous institution
- Character certificate
- Migration certificate (if applicable)
- Passport size photographs (6 copies)
- ID proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)
- Income certificate (for scholarship)

Admission Deadlines:
- Engineering: May 1st - July 31st
- Business Programs: June 1st - August 31st
- Arts and Sciences: July 1st - September 30th
- Diploma Programs: Rolling admissions

Entrance Exams:
- Engineering: JEE Main scores accepted
- MBA: CAT/MAT/XAT scores accepted
- MCA: NIMCET scores accepted
- Other programs: College entrance exam

Admission Fees:
- Application Fee: $50 (non-refundable)
- Registration Fee: $200 (one-time)
- Caution Money: $500 (refundable)
- Identity Card: $25

Hostel Fee: $1200 per semester
Food Plan: $800 per semester (optional)

International Students:
- Additional processing fee: $200
- Visa assistance provided
- English language proficiency required
- International student orientation program
